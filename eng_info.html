History

(Full 10) was founded in 2020 as a staffing company that aims to give young people better first job experiences. The company was founded by five members who all became important employees of the company.

Objectives

The main goal of the company is to enable young people to have the best possible jobs and work experience, which would leave them with a good starting point for the future. The company's other goals are to find people with fast jobs.
